
    Paris is the capital and most populous city of France. It is located in the north-central part of the country.
    France is a country in Western Europe. It has a rich history and culture, and is known for its art, cuisine, and fashion.
    The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris. It is named after the engineer Gustave Eiffel, whose company designed and built the tower.
